Korat

The Korat cat is a breed of domestic cat that is short-haired, silver-tipped, and blue-grey. Also, the Korat cat breed has a small to medium build with a low percentage of body weight, however they are unsually heavy for their size. They are intelligent, playful, active, and very vocal cats.

British Shorthair

The British Shorthair is a cat originating from the United Kingdom with a stocky body, dense coat, and broad coat. This breed can be a wide range of colors and patterns, including its most commonly recognized color variant "British Blue" that has grey-blue coat, orange eyes, and a medium sized tail.

Balinese

The Balinese cat is a long haired breed of domestic cat originating in the United States. They have a Siamese style point coloration and sapphire blue eyes. This breed comes from the natural mutation of the Siamese breed and therefore share many of the same traits being sociable, vocal, playful, inquisitive, and intelligent.

Bengal

The Bengal cat breed is a domestic breed originatin gin the United States from the hybrid of different domestic cat breeds. This breed has a wild appearance, their gold shimmer coming from their leopard cat ancestry and their coats may have may show different marking such as spots, rosettes, arrowhead markings, or marbling. They are an energetic breed which needs a lot of exercise and play time.

Burmese

The Burmese cat breed originates in Burma and is a breed of domestic cat. Burmese cats originally were all dark brown, however they are currently available in many different colors. This breed of cat is known for being uniquely social, playful, and vocal.
